Tiger Facts

Tigers are an endangered species and the largest cat species in the world. These animals are featured prominently in ancient myths and folklore, and continue to be one of the worlds most easily recognized animals. The tiger has many living relatives including the lion, leopard, and jaguar, and it is believed that northwestern China is the origin of their lineage. In time tigers grew in size, surpassing their relatives in order to better hunt their prey of other land mammals.
